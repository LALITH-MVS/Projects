Golden Triangle Tour

Golden Triangle tour of India is a popular tourist circuit that covers the three most beautiful and historic cities of North India - Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur. It is a perfect tour package for those who are interested in exploring India's heritage, culture, and monuments. This tour provides a unique opportunity to experience the richness and diversity of Indian culture and architecture. The Golden Triangle tour starts from Delhi, the capital city of India, which is home to numerous historical and modern monuments. From here, you will be taken to Agra, the city of the Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World. The next stop is Jaipur, the Pink City, known for its majestic forts, palaces, and rich cultural heritage.
